About this site

Klook offers a Klook Pass Taipei that allows access to 2-5 activities in Taipei for a bundle price. There are two types of passes, Standard and Premium, and each pass is valid for 2-5 activities. A Standard pass is only valid for 19 Standard activities, while a Premium pass is valid for an additional 12 Premium activities.

This is a simple tool to help you:

  1. Choose which activities you are interested in
  2. Figure out which pass can be used for your chosen activties
  3. Calculate how much you can save with the pass or if you should book your chosen activities seperately

How to use this site

  1. Look through the list of activities in the table below or on the Klook Pass Taipei webpage.
  2. Choose two or more activities by checking the checkbox in the Choice column in the Choices table. Your choices will show up in the Chosen activities section. To remove a chosen activity, uncheck the checkbox beside the activity name. You can only select a maximum of 5 activities.
  3. The Suggested pass section will recommend a pass, as well as the potential savings over booking each activity seperately.
